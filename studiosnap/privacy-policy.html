Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how StudioSnap ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

Photos and Images

When you use the App to restore a photo, you select an image from your device. That image is transmitted to our AI processing service (Replicate) solely to perform the restoration. We do not store your original or restored images on our servers beyond the time required to process them.

Account Information

To purchase credits and access full-quality restorations, you sign in using Google (Android) or Apple (iOS) authentication. We receive a user identifier and email address from these providers to create and manage your account. We do not receive your password.

Purchase Information

Credit purchases are handled by the App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. We do not process or store your payment card details. We receive transaction records (e.g. which credit pack was purchased) to grant credits to your account.

Usage Data and Analytics

We collect anonymised usage data via Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used and to improve it. This includes events such as app opens, restoration completions, and paywall views. We do not use this data to identify you personally.

Crash Reports

If the App crashes, we collect crash report data via Firebase Crashlytics. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Crash reports may include device model, OS version, and a stack trace of the error.

Restoration History

A record of your restorations (including image file references and timestamps) is stored locally on your device using a local database. This data does not leave your device except as described above.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and return restored images
  • To manage your account and credit balance
  • To fulfil in-app purchases
  • To improve the App through anonymised analytics
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues via crash reports

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with the following third-party service providers as necessary to operate the App:

  • Replicate – AI image processing. Images are transmitted for processing and not retained.
  • RevenueCat – In-app purchase management.
  • Google Firebase – Authentication, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Apple / Google – Authentication and payment processing via their respective platforms.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as you have an account with us. Restoration history is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to or restrict its processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
