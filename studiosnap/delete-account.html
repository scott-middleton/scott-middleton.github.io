Delete Your Account

StudioSnap

You can delete your StudioSnap account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion and what data is affected.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the StudioSnap app on your device
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the top corner
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Your account will be deleted immediately upon confirmation.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your user account (Google or Apple user identifier)
  • Your email address
  • Your credit balance
  • Purchase history records linked to your account

What Data Is Retained

  • Restoration history on your device – Stored locally only. Uninstall the app to remove it.
  • Anonymised analytics data – Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics (e.g. app opens, feature usage) may be retained for service improvement.
  • Crash reports – Non-identifiable technical data (device model, OS version) retained by Firebase Crashlytics.
  • App Store / Google Play purchase records – These are managed by Apple or Google, not by us.

Alternative: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

middleton.apps@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.
